About Sahjanand Granthyagn

Sahajanand Granthyagn is an app for Shri Sahajanand Rural Development Trust, Bhuj / Sahajanand Bookstore, Bhuj / Sahajanand Library, Bhuj. In this app, there are 5000+ books from 40+ publishers written by Indian as well as Foreign authors. Here at Shri Sahajanand Rural Development Trust, Bhuj, books are given with a minimum of 15% discount to a maximum of 70% discount. Here you will find almost all the books from Gujarati literature. You can also find books translated to Gujarati of Foreign authors. This app is a first of its kind, as it sells books online for a library at discounted rates.

  • New Arrivals

    It shows a list of books with discounted price as well as original price. You can search books by book title, author, or publication.

  • Best Sellers

    It shows a list of best-selling books at the store.

  • Sort Books

    Here books are sorted according to the title of a book, author name, bestseller, a discount from low to high or vice-a-versa, price from low to high or vice-a-versa.

  • Favorite List

    You can make any books your favorite for future referral by just clicking on the star provided at the upper right corner.

  • Apply filter on result found

    Shows list of books with title, author, publication, and price with a discount applied on it. You can apply a filter to make your search easier

  • Shopping Cart

    You can as many books you want to your shopping cart.

  • Books Details Information

    It includes title, author, price, discounted price. It contains useful information like language in which book is written, and publisher.

  • Sharing info

    You can share information with your friends and family members using any of the media sharing apps available on your phone.

Some Basic Features

Shows list of books with title, author, publication, and price with a discount applied on it. You can apply a filter to make your search easier.

Filters include

Publication
Here you can select your favorite publisher and make your search easier.
Price
Here you can select book price range for you to purchase
Languages
Here you can select any of the three Indian Language for the books selection - Gujarati, Hindi, English
Discount
Discount filter contains from 0% to 75%+ discount
Author
Select your favorite author
Tag
Here you can select books based on the categories like., religious, fiction/non-fiction novel, autobiography,etc.

Shopping Cart

You can as many books you want to your shopping cart. It will show information like the book title, book author, discounted price, original price, amount of discount you got from the book. You can also remove item/s from your shopping cart and cancel the order. Ordered will be shipped to the user according to the address provided in the Order Form.
